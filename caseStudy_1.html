EZcape

The aim of this project was to design a travel mobile app to help users with limited planning time and budget to organize and plan their trip efficiently that meets their social media exposure needs and budget requirements

My role

    UX researcher
    UX designer
    UI designer

Research

Data was collected by conducting a mixture of phone interviews as well as sending out google forms of questions to friends and family. Data was collected then synthesized using affinity map and empathy map.

Problem Statement

How might we help young users with limited planning time and budget to organize and plan their trip efficiently that meets their social media exposure needs and budget requirement?

Ideation and Brainstorming

Feature Prioritization

User Flow

User flow chart was created based on feature prioritization matrix, brainstorming results, user scenario and competitor analysis.

Lo-Fi Wireframe Prototype & Userbility Test

A low-fidelity wireframe prototype was created in Figma with interactions based on the iterated user flow, then tested by four users. The result was promising but still has room for improvement.

Iterated Hi-Fi prototype for iPhone X

The high-fidelity prototype was created and iterated based on userbility test feedback and accessibility requirements, theme and UI elements were added.
