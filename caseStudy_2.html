Thrive Kids

More than two thirds of parents surveyed say they are likely or very likely to use a platform dedicated to finding activities for their children. As such, we believe that creating a hub, that allows users to filter their choice of activity, by age, distance, and cost would help parents keep their children busy and engaged, in turn helping their kids thrive.

Research

Data was collected by conducting a mixture of phone interviews as well as sending out google forms of questions to parents of young children. Results showing Parents of young children struggle to find extracurricular activities that are accessible and affordable.

Our Solution

More than two thirds of parents surveyed say they are likely or very likely to use a platform dedicated to finding activities for their children. As such, we believe that creating a hub, that allows users to filter their choice of activity, by age, distance, and cost would help parents keep their children busy and engaged, in turn helping their kids thrive.

Branding

User Flow

User flow chart was created based on brainstorming results, user scenario and competitor analysis.

Lo-to-Mid Fi Wireframe Prototype & Userbility Test

A low-fidelity wireframe prototype was created in Invision and by hand sketch based on the iterated user flow, then the iterated Mid-fidelity prototype was tested by users. The result was promising but still has room for improvement.

Iterated Hi-Fi prototype for mobile and desktop

The high-fidelity prototype was created and iterated based on userbility test feedback and accessibility requirements, theme and UI elements were added.

Final product created on Webflow
